About Me

Catwoman is a fictional character created by Bill Finger and Bob Kane who appears in American comic books published by DC Comics, commonly in association with superhero Batman. The character made her debut as "the Cat" in Batman #1, and her real name is Selina Kyle. Though most modern iterations of the character are just as heroic as they are villainous, Catwoman is still commonly recognized as a villan by most viewers and fans, representing Batman's chief female nemesis. Nevertheless, Catwoman is still sometimes an ally to Batman and also is often his lover.
